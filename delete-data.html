Request data deletion

Use this flow to request deletion of personal data while keeping account access where policy allows.

If an account exists for this email, we will send a confirmation link. For privacy, the response is the same either way.

What this does

After confirmation, we delete personal profile data and user-owned personal records. Your login may remain active if required for account integrity and compliance.

Retention: minimal non-personal audit/security records may be retained up to 180 days.

Processing time: normally immediate to 7 days depending on background cleanup.
