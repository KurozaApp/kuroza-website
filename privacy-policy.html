Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026
Last Updated: February 26, 2026

Kuroza AI ("Kuroza", "we", "our", "us") provides social planning features including messaging, event planning, availability scheduling, friend connections, and AI-assisted recommendations. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, how long we keep it, and your rights.

1. Who We Are

Controller/Operator: Kuroza
Contact Email: contact@kuroza.co.uk

2. Information We Collect

A) Information You Provide

  • Account data: Full name, username, email address, password (handled by Firebase Authentication).
  • Sign-up verification data: Verification code and expiry details for email verification.
  • Profile data: Bio, profile picture.
  • Social data: Friend requests, group memberships.
  • Chat data: Message content, including AI-related prompts and replies.
  • Event data: Event title, description, date/time, participants, RSVP status, and event locations.
  • Availability data: Weekly routine, specific available dates, and custom date/time windows.

B) Information Collected Automatically

  • Device identifiers: Push token (FCM/APNs token) to deliver notifications.
  • Presence indicators: Online/offline status and "last seen" timestamps.
  • App activity: Notification and unread status data; basic product analytics events (Firebase Analytics).
  • Service logs: Operational telemetry for reliability and abuse prevention.

C) Location and Place-related Data

When you search for or attach locations to events, we process:

  • Search data: Place name/address, map URL, place ID, photos, and ratings metadata.
  • Precise Location: If you grant the app permission, we may process your GPS (latitude/longitude) to suggest nearby venues or show your proximity to an event. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

D) Files and Media

We use Firebase Storage for profile images and group images.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process personal data to:

  • Create and secure user accounts and verify identities.
  • Enable core social features: messaging, groups, and friend connections.
  • Execute scheduling logic and manage event invitations/RSVPs.
  • Provide AI-assisted chat and automated planning functionality.
  • Deliver push notifications and maintain app security (Firebase App Check).
  • Improve app reliability and enforce technical/rate limits.

4. Legal Bases (UK/EU Users)

Where applicable, we rely on:

  • Contract: To provide the social and AI features you request.
  • Legitimate Interests: Security, fraud prevention, and product reliability.
  • Consent: For optional features like GPS location access and push notifications.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with lawful requests or safety regulations.

5. Third-Party Services / Processors

We use third-party infrastructure and APIs to operate Kuroza:

  • Infrastructure: Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Functions, Messaging, Storage, Analytics, App Check).
  • AI Services: Google Cloud Vertex AI.
  • Mapping: Google Places / Maps APIs.
  • Communications: SendGrid (for verification emails).

These providers process data on our behalf according to their own privacy commitments and our Data Processing Agreements (DPAs).

6. AI Processing & Training

When you use Kuroza AI, your prompts and related context (chat history, availability, or event details) are processed by AI services to generate responses.

  • No Training on Private Data: We do not use your private messages, prompts, or personal planning data to train foundational third-party AI models (such as those provided by Google Vertex AI).
  • Verification: AI outputs may be incorrect or incomplete. Always verify critical details (time, location, participant availability) before acting.

7. Data Sharing & Visibility

A) User-to-User Visibility

Kuroza is a social platform. By using it, certain data is shared with others:

  • Profile Visibility: Your username and profile picture are visible to friends and members of groups you join.
  • Event/Group Data: When you join a group or event, your RSVP status and messages are visible to all other participants in that group/event.

B) External Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only:

  • With the Service Providers listed in Section 5.
  • To comply with legal processes or to protect the safety of our users.
  • In connection with a business transfer (e.g., merger), subject to lawful safeguards.

8. International Transfers

Your data is processed in countries outside your own (primarily the United States) by our infrastructure providers. For transfers from the UK/EEA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and the Data Privacy Framework to ensure your data remains protected to an equivalent standard.

9. Data Retention & Account Deletion

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide services and operate securely.

Account Deletion

You can request account deletion in-app (Profile → Delete Account). This process:

  • Deletes your user document in Firestore.
  • Deletes your Firebase Authentication account.

Important Note on Retention

  • Shared Content: To maintain the integrity of shared social spaces, messages you sent to group chats or details of events you created may remain visible to other participants. In such cases, your personal identifiers will be removed or the content will be attributed to a "Deleted User."
  • Operational Records: We may retain limited logs and metadata for a set period where required by law or for legitimate security and fraud-prevention needs.

10. Security

We use technical and organizational controls, including Firebase App Check and encrypted transmission, to protect your data. However, no method of digital storage or transmission is 100% secure.

11. Children

Kuroza is not intended for children under 13 (or higher as required by local law). If we learn we have collected data from a child without authorization, we will delete it immediately.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, Correct, or Delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Data Portability (request a copy of your data).
  • Withdraw Consent for features like GPS tracking or notifications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@kuroza.co.uk. We will respond to your request within 30 days. We may require you to verify your identity via your registered email before processing the request.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via an in-app notice or email. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision.
