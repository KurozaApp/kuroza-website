Delete your Kuroza account

Follow these steps in the app to permanently delete your account.

Delete in the app

  1. Open Profile.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Enter your password and confirm.
  4. Your account is deleted and you are signed out.

What gets deleted

  • Your Kuroza login account (Firebase Auth).
  • Your user profile in Firestore.
  • Your profile picture and owned group images.
  • Your chat and group membership links.
  • Your event participation data. Events you created are cancelled.

What may be kept

We may retain minimal legal, fraud-prevention, and security records for up to 180 days where required or permitted by law.

Processing time

Deletion starts right after confirmation and is usually completed within 7 days.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app and need support with deletion, contact contact@kuroza.co.uk.
