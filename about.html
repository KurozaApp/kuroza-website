About Kuroza

Bringing Friends Together

We believe staying connected with people who matter should feel effortless, not like project management.

Our mission

Kuroza removes the friction from group planning by combining availability matching, smart suggestions, and clear coordination in one place.

The goal is simple: spend less time organizing and more time making real plans happen.

What problem we solve

Planning fatigue

Too many messages and no clear outcome.

Schedule mismatch

Hard to find real overlap when everyone is busy.

Scattered decisions

Details are spread across apps and get lost.

Our story

Kuroza started from a common frustration: coordinating a group should not take more effort than the event itself.

Built by students, it was first shaped around campus life where schedules constantly change. That same challenge exists everywhere, from friend groups to teams with packed calendars.

Today, Kuroza is focused on turning fragmented planning into a simple flow that helps people spend more time together.
