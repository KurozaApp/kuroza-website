Our Story

Kuroza was born out of frustration. Our founders were constantly battling the same problem that millions face: how do you actually get a group of friends together when everyone has different schedules?

Built by students, for students, Kuroza was created with the university experience in mind—where friendships are everything but calendars are chaotic. But we quickly realized this problem wasn't limited to campus. Whether you're juggling work, family, or a side hustle, staying socially connected shouldn't require a project manager.

Today, Kuroza is helping groups of all kinds spend less time planning and more time doing what matters: making memories together.
