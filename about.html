About Kuroza

Kuroza was born out of a simple but universal frustration: staying connected with friends shouldn't be this difficult.

Between busy schedules, clashing commitments, and endless group chats that never lead anywhere, planning a get-together often feels harder than the hangout itself. We created Kuroza to change that.

At its core, Kuroza is a social organiser that removes the stress from planning events and brings back the joy of spending time with the people who matter.

By using smart suggestions based on your group's availability and preferences, Kuroza helps you skip the back-and-forth messages and jump straight to making memories.

Whether it's a quick coffee, a study session, or a full weekend trip, the app makes it effortless to turn ideas into reality.

Unlike traditional event apps, Kuroza is designed for everyday use. It's not just for birthdays or big nights out—it's for the smaller, more meaningful moments that strengthen friendships over time. Our focus is on simplicity, reliability, and making social life feel natural again in a world where it's easy to drift apart.

Built by students, for students, Kuroza was created with the university experience in mind, where friendships are everything and schedules change constantly. But our vision reaches far beyond campus. We imagine Kuroza becoming the go-to tool for anyone who wants to stay socially connected without the hassle, no matter where they are in the world.

Kuroza is more than an app—it's a philosophy: time together should never be lost to endless planning. By blending technology with the human need for connection, we're making social life simple again.

Join us on this journey to make hanging out with friends as easy as it should be. Because at the end of the day, it's the moments we share that truly matter.
