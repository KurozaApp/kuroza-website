Modern Slavery Statement

Effective Date: March 8, 2026
Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Our Commitment

Kuroza is a small startup company committed to ethical business practices. We have zero tolerance for modern slavery and human trafficking in our business and supply chains.

Our Business

Kuroza develops social planning software. We operate as a lean team, primarly based in the UK, and exclusively use established, reputable services for our operations.

Our Service Providers

We use established, well-regulated third-party service providers for hosting, infrastructure, and development tools. All our service providers maintain robust compliance frameworks and policies regarding labor practices and ethical sourcing.

Risk Assessment

Given our business model and the nature of our service providers, the risk of modern slavery in our operations is extremely low. We do not manufacture goods, maintain large supply chains, or engage in labor-intensive practices. Our employees are directly employed and based in the UK.

If you have any concerns about this statement or our practices, please contact us.
