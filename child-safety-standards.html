Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: March 8, 2026
Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Kuroza has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including child sexual abuse material (CSAM). We prohibit any content, behavior, or activity that exploits, endangers, or sexually harms children.

1. Prohibited Content and Behavior

  • Any CSAM content or links to CSAM.
  • Sexual exploitation, grooming, or predatory behavior involving minors.
  • Attempts to solicit, share, or distribute child exploitative content.
  • Any use of Kuroza to facilitate abuse, trafficking, or exploitation of children.

2. Reporting and In-App Safety

Kuroza provides in-app reporting tools so users can report child safety concerns. We review reports promptly and take enforcement action, including removing content, restricting features, suspending accounts, or permanently banning users.

3. Escalation to Authorities

Where required, Kuroza reports relevant incidents to appropriate regional or national authorities, including child protection and law enforcement bodies.

4. Compliance Commitment

Kuroza complies with applicable child safety laws and platform requirements, and continuously updates moderation and safety processes to reduce abuse risks.
